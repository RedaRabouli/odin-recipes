Briwat

Description

Briwat are savory Moroccan pastries filled with a variety of ingredients like meat, cheese, or vegetables. They're made by wrapping the fillings in thin, crispy pastry dough and then frying until golden brown.

Ingredients:

  • Warka pastry sheets
  • Ground beef or lamb
  • Onion
  • Parsley
  • Spices (cumin, paprika, cinnamon)
  • Eggs (optional)
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Steps:

  1. Prepare the filling
  2. Cut the pastry sheets into triangles
  3. Place a spoonful of filling on each triangle
  4. Fold the pastry over the filling and seal
  5. Fry the briwat until golden brown
  6. Drain on paper towels and serve hot
