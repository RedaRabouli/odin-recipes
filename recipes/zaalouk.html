Zaalouk

Description

Zaalouk is a delicious Moroccan eggplant dip or relish. It's made by slow-cooking eggplant with tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, and a blend of spices. The result is a rich, flavorful spread that's often served as an appetizer with Moroccan bread.

Ingredients:

  • Eggplants
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Parsley
  • Salt and pepper
